Privacy Policy

Negativity Tracker  ·  The Maestro Studios  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Summary: Negativity Tracker does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. No account registration is required. The only data processed externally relates to your subscription, which is handled entirely by Google Play.

1. Who We Are

Negativity Tracker is developed and published by The Maestro Studios, an independent mobile app studio based in Poland, European Union. For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at hercainyee@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the app.

Data stored locally on your device

  • Negativity reports — entries you create within the app, including categories (brands, places, software), timestamps, and analysis results. This data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • App preferences and settings — your in-app configuration, badge progress, and tree growth stage. Stored locally on your device.
  • Google Play Games backup (optional) — if you enable Google Play Games Services, your badge progress and tree stage may be backed up to your Google account via the GPGS Saved Games API. This backup is governed by Google's privacy policy, not ours.

Data processed by third parties on your behalf

  • Subscription and billing data — if you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive only a non-personal subscription token confirming your active tier. We do not receive or store your payment card details, name, or billing address.
  • Content analysis — when you run a report, the subject of your query (e.g. a brand name or app name) is sent to third-party APIs (including AWS Comprehend and xAI) for sentiment analysis. No personal data about you is included in these requests.
  • Public data sources — the app retrieves publicly available content (Reddit posts, news articles, app store reviews, etc.) relevant to your query. This retrieval does not involve any personal data.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
  • Any data from children — the app is not directed at users under 13

4. How Your Data Is Used

Data stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Display your reports, history, and analysis results within the app
  • Track your badge progress and tree growth stage
  • Determine your subscription tier and available monthly reports

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described below:

  • Google Play / Google LLC — handles subscription billing and, if enabled, GPGS backup. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • AWS (Amazon Web Services) — processes anonymised query text for sentiment analysis. Subject to AWS's Privacy Notice.
  • xAI — processes anonymised query text for report generation. Subject to xAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by applicable law or a valid legal process.

6. Data Retention

All data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the app or clear its data manually. We do not operate servers that retain your personal data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a data controller established in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, most GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure) are exercised by managing data directly on your device. However, you have the right to:

  • Request confirmation of whether we hold any personal data about you
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (in Poland: UODO — uodo.gov.pl)

To exercise any rights or ask questions, contact us at hercainyee@gmail.com.

8. International Users

The app is available globally. Since all personal processing occurs locally on your device and we do not operate data servers, no cross-border data transfers of personal data take place on our part. Third-party processors (Google, AWS, xAI) operate under their own international data transfer mechanisms.

9. Children's Privacy

Negativity Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

10. Security

All data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's own security mechanisms. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date. We do not transmit personal data over the internet, which eliminates the most common categories of data breach risk.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
